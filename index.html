What is Deepfake?

Deepfakes are AI-generated media where a person's face, voice, or actions are altered to create highly realistic but fake content. These manipulated videos and images can be used for misinformation, fraud, or entertainment.

How Our System Works

Our deepfake recognition system uses advanced machine learning and AI algorithms to analyze videos and images. It detects subtle inconsistencies in facial expressions, lighting, and voice modulation to identify potential deepfakes.
